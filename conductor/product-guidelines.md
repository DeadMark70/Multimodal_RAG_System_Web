# Product Guidelines

## Visual Identity
- **Professional & Clean:** Minimalist design with high contrast, focusing on readability and data visualization.
- **Dark Mode Support:** User-selectable Dark Mode to accommodate different working environments and preferences.

## Tone of Voice
- **Professional & Objective:** The application interface and feedback should be neutral, fact-based, and concise.
  *Note: AI response tone is managed backend-side, but frontend UI copy should align with this professional standard.*

## User Experience (UX)
### Long-Running Operations
- **Detailed Status Indicators:** Display clear loading states with specific phase labels (e.g., "Searching...", "Synthesizing...") to inform users of system activity.
- **Background Processing:** Enable users to navigate away from active tasks and receive notifications upon completion, preventing workflow blocking.

### Data Visualization
- **Progressive Disclosure:** Present high-level summaries initially, providing mechanisms for users to drill down into granular details on demand.
